Chocolate Soy Milk

Description

Chocolate Soy Milk recipe or Chocolate Flavored soy milk recipe. This is made with chocolate, honey and milk.

Ingredients

  • 500g soy milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • Maple syrup to taste
  • 3 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Whisk cocoa powder, maple syrup and water until creamy.
  2. Blend soy milk and the previous cocoa mix until creamy.
  3. Serve cold.
